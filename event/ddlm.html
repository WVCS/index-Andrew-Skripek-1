    • Dia De Los Muertos

    Dia De Los Muertos, or The day of the dead, is one of the most important festivities in Mexico because Mexicans honor their dead with a joyful and positive celebration, turned into a fantastic popular holiday.
    (Día de los Muertos is celebrated on All Saints Day and All Souls Day, minor holidays in the Catholic calendar.) Assured that the dead would be insulted by mourning or sadness, Día de los Muertos celebrates the lives of the deceased with food, drink, parties, and activities the dead enjoyed in life.
